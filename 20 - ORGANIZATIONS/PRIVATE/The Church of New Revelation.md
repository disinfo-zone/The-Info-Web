The Church of New Revelation was a sham religious entity incorporated in New Jersey that served as an umbrella organization for child exploitation operations during the 1970s. It was created solely on paper by [[Dyer Grossman]] to gain tax-exempt status for affiliated programs. [[Father Bud's Boys Farm]] in Monteagle, Tennessee was incorporated under the Church of New Revelation, as was [[Brother Paul's Children's Mission]] on [[North Fox Island]]. The church was described by authorities as a fraudulent referral agency for pornography distribution rather than a legitimate religious organization. [[Francis Shelden]] and [[Adam Starchild]] were connected to the church through their roles in incorporating the North Fox Island mission.[^1]
