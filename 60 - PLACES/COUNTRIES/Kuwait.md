[[Kuwait]] is a country in the Middle East. [[Lester Coleman]]'s affidavit states that the [[DEA|DEA]] was using [[Euramae Trading Company]] to sell [[PROMIS]] software to drug abuse control agencies in various Middle Eastern countries, including [[Kuwait]].[^1]
