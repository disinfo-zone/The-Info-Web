The 2019 AWD Nuclear Congress was an in-person leadership gathering of [[Atomwaffen Division]] held in [[Las Vegas, Nevada]] in approximately September 2019 and organized by [[Joshua Caleb Sutter]], then serving as a paid [[FBI]] informant whose FBI-provided funds covered member travel and lodging. [[James Mason]], author of *[[Siege]]* and the foundational ideological figure of [[Siege Culture|siege culture]] accelerationism, attended and delivered speeches - his first documented in-person appearance with AWD. A propaganda video posted on September 30, 2019 (the birthday of Nazi figure [[Savitri Devi]]) publicized the event. The gathering led directly to [[Operation Erste Saule]], the media harassment campaign that resulted in the February 2020 federal arrests of [[Kaleb Cole]], [[Cameron Shea]], and others.[^1]

### Organization and Funding

Sutter organized the Nuclear Congress under his alias "swissdiscipline." His FBI handlers were aware of and funded member attendance; the FBI payments to Sutter that covered this period totaled $78,133.20 plus expense advances from February 7, 2018 onward, in addition to prior payments stretching back to 2003. This means that federal funds paid by the FBI to its informant were used to organize and facilitate AWD's primary leadership gathering of 2019. This dimension of the Nuclear Congress became public through court documents in the federal prosecution of Kaleb Cole, when motion-to-suppress filings established Sutter's informant status in 2021.[^2]

### Attendees and Content

The propaganda video shows Mason standing with approximately 12 AWD members in military uniforms bearing AWD patches, all masked. Mason spoke about the necessity of murder and "small lone wolf terror attacks" against the government. Members delivered prepared speeches "theorizing about the way forward for the group." The video's binary code translated to "select presentations to be presented to the public," indicating additional content existed beyond the released footage.

Mason's speeches were later published in full on the American Futurist website under the title "The Nuclear Congress Transcripts: Philosophy of Siege." The September 30 release date was chosen to coincide with Savitri Devi's birthday, a calendar date significant in the neo-Nazi occultist tradition AWD had adopted through its ToB relationship.

Mason's Nuclear Congress appearance was notable as his first public in-person meeting with AWD, translating his status as the intellectual godfather of *Siege* culture into a documented physical presence at the organization's leadership summit.

### Operation Erste Saule

Following the Nuclear Congress, [[Cameron Shea]] created an encrypted [[Wire]] messaging group named "[[Operation Erste Saule]]" (German: "First Pillar") to coordinate a campaign of harassment and intimidation against journalists and civil rights organizations who had reported on AWD. The Nuclear Congress is explicitly named in the criminal complaint affidavit for Kaleb Cole's arrest (Western District of Washington, February 26, 2020) as the gathering after which the conspiracy to harass media figures was formulated.

The subsequent campaign - mailing threatening posters bearing swastikas and phrases including "We know where you live" and "Death to Pigs" to journalists and Jewish activists - led to the simultaneous federal arrests in multiple states in February 2020 that effectively dismantled AWD's national leadership.[^3]
