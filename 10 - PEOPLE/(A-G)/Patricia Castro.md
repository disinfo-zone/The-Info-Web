[[Patricia Castro]] was a friend of [[Fred Alvarez]] and a victim of an execution-style triple homicide on June 29, 1981, in Rancho Mirage, [[California]]. She was murdered along with [[Fred Alvarez]] and [[Ralph Boger]].[^1]
