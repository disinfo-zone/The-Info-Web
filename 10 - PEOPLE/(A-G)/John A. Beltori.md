John A. Beltori was identified by [[INSLAW|Inslaw]] as a witness whose testimony provided circumstantial evidence of a conspiracy involving [[Earl Brian]] and [[Department of Justice|DOJ]] officials to steal the [[PROMIS]] software. The Special Counsel's investigation, however, found no credible evidence to support this claim.[^1]

[^1]: U.S. Department of Justice. *Report of Special Counsel Nicholas J. Bua to the Attorney General of the United States Regarding the Allegations of Inslaw, Inc.* March 1993.
