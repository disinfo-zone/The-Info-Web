Richard Dwyer was a [[CIA]] agent who was present at [[Jonestown]], [[Guyana]], during the 1978 mass deaths of over 900 members of the [[People's Temple]]. In a recording made during the final moments of the mass deaths, [[Jim Jones]] could be heard shouting, "Get Dwyer out of here," explicitly naming the CIA operative as the killings unfolded.[^1]

Dwyer's presence at Jonestown was one of several pieces of evidence pointing to CIA involvement in the People's Temple operation. Researchers documented a strong CIA presence in almost all of the dealings of Jim Jones before, during, and after Jonestown. Dwyer was working under diplomatic cover at the time of the massacre, which allowed him to be present at the remote Guyana site. His identification by Jones on the death tape, combined with the discovery of drugs used in known [[MKULTRA]] operations at the site, including 11,000 doses of Thorazine, contributed to theories that the Jonestown massacre was a CIA mind control experiment rather than a mass suicide.[^1]
