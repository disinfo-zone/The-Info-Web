[[Hector Bermudez]], aliases "Fiasco," "Rogue," and "Diablo," is a resident of [[Queens, New York]] and a named leader of [[Greggy's Cult]] (also called the Cult of Greg), the online child exploitation network that operated on [[Discord]] from approximately January 2020 through January 2021. He was born approximately 1996 and was 29 at the time of his December 2025 indictment.

### Greggy's Cult and the 764 Lineage

Greggy's Cult operated using methods that would define the [[764 Network|764]] network: recruiting minor victims through gaming platforms and social media, coercing them into producing [[CSAM]], and blackmailing victims with the material to compel ongoing compliance including self-harm. [[NCITE]] tracked Greggy's Cult alongside [[CVLT]] and 764 as three connected networks accounting for 31 individuals publicly charged with federal crimes through December 2025. Prominent members of 764 and related successor networks were also members of Greggy's Cult, per the DOJ's December 2025 press release.[^1]

### Federal Charges (United States v. Bermudez, 1:25-cr-00361)

A federal grand jury in the [[Eastern District of New York]] returned an indictment unsealed on December 2, 2025, charging Bermudez alongside four co-defendants: [[Zachary Dosch]] (26, Albuquerque, New Mexico), [[Rumaldo Valdez]] (22, Wahiawa, Hawaii), [[David Brilhante]] (28, San Diego, California), and [[Camden Rodriguez]] (22, Longmont, Colorado). The case number is 1:25-cr-00361 (PKC).[^2]

The 10-count indictment charged: engaging in a child exploitation enterprise, conspiracy to produce child pornography, conspiracy to receive and distribute child pornography, and conspiracy to communicate interstate threats. The alleged criminal conduct covered January 2020 through January 2021.[^2]

No guilty plea or sentencing outcome for Bermudez was publicly reported as of May 2026.
