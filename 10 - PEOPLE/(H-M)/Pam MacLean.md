[[Pam MacLean]] was a reporter for the *San Francisco Daily Journal*. She spent thousands of dollars to get the [[John Di Giorgio]] file unsealed. The unsealed documents revealed that the [[United States|U.S. government]] was looking for an alleged $13 million in laundered drug funds believed to be in [[Switzerland]], [[Germany]], and [[Israel]].[^1]
