Assistant [[U.S. Attorney's office|U.S. Attorney, District of Columbia]] that together with assistant attorney [[Charles R. Work]] helped to design the initial version of [[PROMIS]] with criminologist [[Joan E. Jacoby]] from 1969-1971.

Co-wrote a paper with Work about the process entitled “Developing an Automated Information System for The Prosecutor” published in American Criminal Law Quarterly, Vol 9, No 1, Fall 1970[^1]
