[[William Shockley]] was a [[United States Attorney|U.S. Attorney]] in San Francisco. He reportedly wrote the letter that [[Ben Kalka]] signed, stating that Kalka did not obtain his methamphetamine precursors from the [[United States|U.S. government]]. This letter was found by Detective [[Sue Todd]] in the Di Giorgio Extradition File.[^1]
