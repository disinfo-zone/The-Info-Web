[[Sarcis Sargalian]] was an [[United States|American]] arms dealer of Lebanese-Armenian extraction. He was involved in projects supplying [[Iraq]] with military equipment, working with [[Ihsan Barbouti]] and [[Richard Babayan]].[^1]
